## Key Features

Let's take a closer look at the key features that set OpenArmor apart:

### eBPF Logging

Our eBPF logging technology allows you to capture kernel-level system logs with minimal overhead, providing you with deep observability into your system's operations. With OpenArmor, you can gain invaluable insights into what's happening behind the scenes on your network.

### OCSF Standardization

We believe in the power of standardized data for effective cybersecurity. That's why we structure and normalize logs into the Open Cyber Security Schema Format (OCSF), ensuring interoperability and ease of analysis across different platforms and systems.

### AI Log Processing

OpenArmor leverages artificial intelligence algorithms to analyze and transform logs into actionable insights. By harnessing the power of AI, we enable you to detect and respond to cyber threats more efficiently than ever before.

### Automated Threat Detection

Our machine learning algorithms baseline normal behavior and identify anomalies indicative of cyber threats, enabling proactive monitoring and threat detection. With OpenArmor, you can stay one step ahead of potential security breaches.
